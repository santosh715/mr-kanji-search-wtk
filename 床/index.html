On-Yomi: ショウ — Kun-Yomi: とこ、ゆか

Koohii stories:

1) [mspertus] 21-6-2006(216): The two safe places to sleep in the wilderness are in a tree and in a cave. So these can be considered as beds.

2) [indigo] 11-2-2008(102): (based on mspertus:) The two safe places to sleep in the wilderness are in a tree and in a cave. So to be on the safe side it is best to put a tree in a cave and use it as a bed.

3) [Tornadic_Indignation] 2-5-2009(89): When you take a girl to bed, your tree goes in her cave.

4) [amontice] 25-10-2007(19): The only good way to sleep in a bed in the wilderness is to chop down a tree, drag it into a cave and carve it up so you can have some shelter from the elements.

5) [kathodos] 23-2-2009(17): Draculas bed is a wooden coffin in a cave.
