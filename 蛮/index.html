barbarian

Reading:

On-Yomi: バン — Kun-Yomi: えびす

Koohii stories:

1) [adrianbarritt] 3-5-2008(278): The barbarian picks an apple from above and eats it even though there is a worm (insect) in it.

2) [Raichu] 3-4-2007(39): Barbarians eat apples with worms still in them.

3) [Aikou] 16-9-2006(28): Barbarian is derived from "barba rossa" which means red beard (true!). In this red beard live lots of disgusting insects.

4) [zaydana] 27-5-2009(17): Imagine a barbarian choosing between two apples - one full of insects, and one without. The barbarian of course decides to eat the one with insects - "I need my meat for the day!".

5) [mistamark] 8-1-2009(4): The barbarian eats the red apple, insect and all!!
