annexed

Reading:

On-Yomi: テン — Kun-Yomi: そ.える、そ.う、も.える、も.う

Koohii stories:

1) [ihatobu] 7-9-2007(229): Hawaii is a little drop of heaven nestled in the waters of the Pacific, most often visited by honeymooners and valentines. It was annexed to the U.S.A. in 1959, and more recently to Japan as well– albeit only unofficially, and through private commercial holdings.

2) [Katsuo] 6-11-2009(95): Tip 1: 天 and 夭 (heavens and sapling) are different, but RTK confuses them. Sapling appears in numbers 634, 938, 2078, 2079, 2080, and all characters containing angel 喬 (429, 430, 1221, 2104, 2120, 2369). Tip 2: The kanji 添 means "something added/attached", so think of it as a building annex. Story: While in the Annex watering saplings, you find a valentine card amongst them – addressed to you!

3) [ruisu] 3-3-2008(60): He drank all the holy water, so god annexed his heart to heaven.

4) [Ceryn] 9-5-2008(51): First learn 忝 【ＧＲＡＴＥＦＵＬ】 (I was very GRATEFUL to have recieved a VALENTINE straight from HEAVEN.) It's a rare kanji used in かたじけない which means grateful. Then 添 【ＡＮＮＥＸＥＤ】 (You should be GRATEFUL the city just ANNEXED some land and is building a new WATER park.).

5) [Spidercat] 21-3-2008(47): You shed tears (water) as your departed valentine is annexed to the heavens.
