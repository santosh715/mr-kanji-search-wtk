Koohii stories:

1) [Aerin] 1-10-2007(152): Mr. T is a harsh taskmaster who will discipline you (beat you into shape) with his walking stick.

2) [fuaburisu] 29-1-2008(30): Note : the meaning is in the positive sense, to cultivate one's character or interests. To work to make yourself or something better.

3) [Istvan] 2-8-2007(24): You may not have known it, but Mr. T is into discispline. Not just self discipline, in order to get into shape, but what he really likes is when the Task master spanks him with her walking stick. Don't tell Mr. T I told you. I pity da fool, and da fool would be me!!!

4) [rtkrtk] 5-2-2008(17): Compare with permanence/remote (#958 悠). Mr. T went to visit his old taskmaster who had a walking stick. Halfway on the way to that remote region, Mr. T was about to give up because of the long and difficult journey when he ran into Bruce Lee, who told him that discipline is needed to reach your goal.

5) [Perry] 13-2-2009(13): Mr. T joins the X-men. When they catch mystique, he beats her with a walking stick like a true taskmaster to discipline her. But it turns out mystique has taken the shape of the walking stick, and she then beats him.
