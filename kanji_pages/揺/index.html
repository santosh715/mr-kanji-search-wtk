Reading:

On-Yomi: ヨウ — Kun-Yomi: ゆ.れる、ゆ.る、ゆ.らぐ、ゆ.るぐ、ゆ.する、ゆ.さぶる、ゆ.すぶる、うご.く

Primitive:

condor Vulture . . . king . . . mountain. By now you should be used to finding two elements double up on a stroke, as is the case here with king and mountain. [9]
