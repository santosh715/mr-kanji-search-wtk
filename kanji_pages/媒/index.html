mediator

Reading:

On-Yomi: バイ — Kun-Yomi: なこうど

Koohii stories:

1) [akrodha] 4-6-2007(214): In a divorce, the woman needs a mediator because she won't talk to that so-and-so. Imagine the woman on the ground and the so-and-so up in the tree, and the poor mediator climbing up and down, trying to mediate their fight.

2) [Raichu] 25-4-2007(72): In a divorce, the woman needs a mediator because she won't talk to that so-and-so.

3) [Filip] 2-3-2008(36): The two little gossiping girls with their lollipops are having a fight. Now an adult woman (mom or teacher) has to come between them an serve as a mediator.

4) [sweetneet] 7-9-2006(12): Well the "so-and-so"girl that was picked on in the previous frame tattled on the catty girls, and a catfight breaks out…at which point yet another woman was brought forth to be the mediator between their squabble. not like that would help things, but whatever…

5) [Renaissance] 18-11-2009(9): (inspired by akrodha, with basketman integrated in the story) Basketman is going through a divorce. The woman needs a mediator because she won't talk to Basketman. Imagine the mediator walking back and forth between the woman and Basketman, trying to understand the stuff Basketman is saying from under his basket.
