alms

Reading:

On-Yomi: シ、セ — Kun-Yomi: ほどこ.す

Primitive:

banner Here we have a unique enclosure made up of two elements: compass and reclining. Think of the banner as a standard for rallying around; then imagine a crowd reclining before a compass (presumably to give them a "direction" in life). [6]

Koohii stories:

2) [Howdoken] 29-3-2008(126): "ALMS for the poor" cries the beggar under his tattered BANNER. An evil bastard instead drops a SCORPION into his begging bowl.

The you take the man to an hospital and give him alms to pay for the cure.
