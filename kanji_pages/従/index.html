accompany

Reading:

On-Yomi: ジュウ、ショウ、ジュ — Kun-Yomi: したが.う、したが.える、より

Koohii stories:

1) [Hyland1] 7-2-2007(294): The line of viking soldiers is always accompanied by a horn mending specialist.

2) [meolox] 17-12-2007(163): I'm going to accompany the horny girl to her room so I can "mend" her.

3) [nest0r] 11-11-2007(91): As you're going along someplace, you pause to mend a wounded animal's horns, and it decides to go with you, to accompany you as a pet/sidekick, out of gratitude.

4) [cerulean] 29-1-2009(72): Japanese Folklore:———– Mend the Horns of an Oni, and he will accompany you on your travels, Going anywhere you choose.

5) [herman] 12-11-2008(23): The symbol on the right works well as Horny Repairman (mender) so you can imagine a porn character like a cable repairman that shows up at the door, and a beautiful girl answers (having seen the Big Lebowski helps with this visual). But instead of coming in, he asks the girl to accompany him to his van and she obeys and goes with him. "obey" is the primary meaning of this kanji so it's good to work it into the visual.
