spirit

Reading:

On-Yomi: キ、ケ — Kun-Yomi: いき

Koohii stories:

1) [dingomick] 17-4-2007(130): Captain Hook, his spirit crushed, is lying on the ground cursing Wolverine who has defeated him with his far superior claws.

2) [Balaam] 2-2-2008(57): You're reclining on the floor trying to hook a sheaf with a small hook through the cracks. That you've been doing this for seven hours without success shows your burning spirit.

3) [mantixen] 11-7-2009(34): Wolverine has a fighting spirit, and even while reclining on the floor sharpens the hooks on his claws.

4) [fuaburisu] 30-5-2006(27): Hint : the first four strokes may be seen as Captain Hook : reclining on the floor, shaking his hook in the air and moaning "Peter Pan! Why did you elude me again! Whyyy!". Se here we have Captain Hook having one of his fits, and about the only thing that will bring him his spirit back is a sheaf. The last part probably won't make sense but I am French speaking and somehow I settled onto the image of a "bouquet" of flowers for the word "sheaf".

5) [Hujis] 22-5-2009(18): Someone is lying on the floor, a huge x ripped into their stomach with a fishhook. A passer by says "Genki desu ka".
