calm

Reading:

On-Yomi: オン — Kun-Yomi: おだ.やか

Koohii stories:

1) [Johnnyltn] 28-2-2007(175): This whole kanji is a picture of calmness. The wheat has been harvested and even the vultures sit calmly watching the humans sweep and clean-up after the harvest is over. Hearts are peaceful and calm at this time of the year. Even the days are calm without storm.

2) [joesan] 29-10-2007(142): The farmer is calm knowing that his wheat fields are protected from vultures by his strawman with a heart (all the better to do the job properly).

3) [stefanot] 14-4-2007(80): A Calm day: the vulture is flying above the wheat fields, where there's a a scarecrow made with a broom with an heart painted on the shirt (like oz scarecrow).

4) [kusterdu] 25-3-2008(42): The farmer lying in the middle of the wheat field is so calm the vultures think he is dead and keep trying to get at his heart. He just lightly brushes them away with a broom, barely noticing their presence.

5) [jameserb] 6-8-2008(35): Creepy but calm bucolic scene: In a field of wheat, a vulture sits atop a scarecrow's broom, calmly eating a heart (a mouse heart?).
