slope

Reading:

On-Yomi: ハン — Kun-Yomi: さか

Primitive:

Ground . . . anti-. [7]

Koohii stories:

1) [jaresty] 8-10-2007(112): As the villain (pushed off by the anti-hero) is tumbling down the slope, he activates gadget skis to escape, spraying soil everywhere as he flies down the slope.

2) [nakome] 4-3-2008(100): This kanji depicts a classic enviornmental problem: erosion. Erosion occurs when all of the soil washes of the side of the cliff, eventually causing the cliff to disappear, leaving a slope (an anti- cliff if you will) in its place.

3) [Dakoina] 2-3-2009(83): A slope is anti-flat soil.

4) [rtkrtk] 7-1-2008(30): Slope = ski slope. You're speeding down the ski slope with your anti-gravity skis. These special skis work not only on snow, they even work on raw soil, allowing you to hover above the ground.

5) [Filip] 28-1-2008(20): Dirty Harry kicks the guy in the crotch. The poor guy rolling down the slope end up in a pile of earth.
