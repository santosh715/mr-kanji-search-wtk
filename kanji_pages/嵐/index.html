storm

Reading:

On-Yomi: ラン — Kun-Yomi: あらし

Primitive:

Mountain . . . winds. [12]

Koohii stories:

1) [raulir] 30-4-2006(250): Storm is a full-blown wind that rips the mountains off the ground (you see one flying here).

2) [fuaburisu] 16-1-2006(76): Winds are gathering at the base of the mountain (note the hint to primitive placement), going up, and turning into a huge mountain storm.

3) [Katsuo] 5-11-2007(73): What a storm! Even a mountain was carried off by the wind!

4) [dingomick] 21-2-2007(15): A storm is winds strong enough to lift a mountain!

5) [LazyNomad] 9-4-2009(6): You are sitting on top of Fuji, enjoying spectacular view of the storm below.
