plump

Reading:

On-Yomi: タイ、タ — Kun-Yomi: ふと.い、ふと.る

Koohii stories:

1) [lilpea] 3-8-2007(152): A large person with a big drop of sweat dripping off of him because he is so plump.

2) [chamcham] 27-2-2006(80): Fat("plump") men always have small penises…..the kanji is a combination of "large" with a drop(representing the small penis between his legs).

3) [Danieru] 28-1-2008(36): A big, plump sumo wrestler with a loincloth covering his little drop. (Thanks Fabrice).

4) [fuaburisu] 5-2-2006(19): To keep this distinct from the primitive meaning of "round" ("fat man") I suggest to use a specific image, such as that of a plump Japanese sumo wrestler, and let the drop between the legs be the loincloth (which compared to their large body size, is quite a drop). To illustrate the importance of this, have a look at the kanji for "tenacious" (frame 1506) and "burdensome" (frame 1986).

5) [ctalbottclark] 8-3-2008(15): The plump dog dropped its tail between its legs.
