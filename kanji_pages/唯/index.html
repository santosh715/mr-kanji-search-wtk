Primitive:

turkey(indyk) This primitive is best remembered as an old turkey, complete with pipe and monocle. Its writing is somewhat peculiar, so take note of the order of the strokes. Let the first four strokes stand for the turkey’s head, neck, and drooping chin. The remainder can then be pictographic of the plumage. [8]
