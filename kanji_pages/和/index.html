Koohii stories:

1) [skinnyneo] 18-10-2007(342): The first step to harmony is having enough wheat to feed every mouth in the world. Once we are all fed it is easier to get a long in harmony.

2) [gorgon] 29-1-2006(40): People who live in harmony with nature live in the country. they are yokels or hillbillys. See Billy-bob sitting on the porch in their denhim dungarees a sheaf of wheat, sticking from their mouth.

3) [Piitaa] 5-6-2006(32): What better image of harmony and peace than relaxing amidst the wheat, with one long stalk in your mouth.

4) [Filip] 20-1-2008(10): A cereal commercial. Look at the family so happy in harmony. You know its true. Early in the morning you feel like smiling at each other, and being very happy while you eat that cereal…. not… I wake up, shuve it in my mouth and off i am.

5) [CountPacula] 8-6-2008(8): Tasty wheat brings true harmony to the mouth.
