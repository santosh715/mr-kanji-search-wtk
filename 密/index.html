secrecy

Reading:

On-Yomi: ミツ — Kun-Yomi: ひそ.か

Koohii stories:

1) [chamcham] 29-4-2007(214): To maintain secrecy, secret societies invariably have to house themselves in the moutains.

2) [dwhitman] 7-11-2007(161): To maintain secrecy, secret societies invariably have to house themselves on top of the moutains. (Slight tweak of chamcham's story, using fuaburisa's idea for remembering where to put the mountain primitive.).

3) [Transtic] 16-1-2008(53): This kanji tells you an original version of "Brokeback Mountain". Here, the couple of cowboys have fallen in love, and their only way to keep their love in SECRECY is by meeting invariably at the house on the top of the mountain.

4) [j0k0m0] 12-4-2009(41): Secrecy in your house, invariably leads to a mountain of trouble.

5) [zazen666] 4-9-2007(17): My lover and I had a house in the mountains so that we could meet in secrecy, but it was invariably found out by my wife.
