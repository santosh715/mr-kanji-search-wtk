dwell

Reading:

On-Yomi: ジュウ、ヂュウ、チュウ — Kun-Yomi: す.む、す.まう、-ず.まい

Koohii stories:

1) [synewave] 3-9-2006(108): When Mr T shows people round his dwelling he takes a big candlestick (with a candle in it!) so that the light shows off his collection of bling bling jewellery.

2) [Danieru] 9-7-2008(75): Mr. T DWELLS in a house fit for a lord. As with all the bling, he has to keep up appearances, you know. Now all the people on his street are just 'trying to keep up with the T's' (as opposed to 'keeping up with the Jones').

3) [fuaburisu] 29-12-2005(52): Mister T dwells in a big brass candlestick, the flame of which serves as a beacon for the boats in the nearby harbour. Sometimes in the morning, Mister T has to clear the wax that has landed and solidified in front of the door.

4) [astridtops] 20-6-2006(27): A person dwells in the place where he puts his candlesticks.

5) [Verdana] 26-5-2009(23): Khatzumoto doesn't dwell on the past when he sucked in Japanese. Instead, he is now lord of Japanese language.
